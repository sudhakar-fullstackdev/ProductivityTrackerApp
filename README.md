# ProductivityTrackerApp
A full-featured personal iOS productivity tracker designed to help you stay on top of your daily routines, time management, and tasks. By maintaining a straightforward day-by-day and hour-by-hour history of what you've achieved, the app computes a productivity score, categorizes your activities, and provides actionable insights for self-improvement.

## 🌟 Features
- **Daily Dashboard**: A high-level view showing productive hours, your average score, and your top category of the day.
- **Hourly Timeline Logging**: Seamlessly input tasks, write notes, and add tags for every hour of your day.
- **Color-coded Categories**: Associate activities with distinct color-coded categories (e.g., Coding, Exercise, Leisure, Work).
- **Analytics & Insights**: Weekly average scores, longest productivity streaks, and clear breakdown charts.
- **Offline Reliability**: Fully functional without an internet connection using a fast local SQLite data layer.

## 🛠 Tech Stack & Architecture
- **Language**: Swift
- **UI Framework**: SwiftUI 
- **Architecture**: MVVM (Model-View-ViewModel) for clean business logic and UI separation. 
- **Database**: Lightweight local native edge SQLite.
- **State Management**: Combine framework (`@Published`, `@StateObject`, `ObservableObject`).
